Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our express delivery services, shipping policies, tracking, and more.

How do I book a delivery or pickup? You can book directly through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service.
Do you offer online booking or virtual services? Yes, all our services can be booked online through our website or app.
What's the difference between standard and express delivery? Standard delivery is cost-effective with regular speed, while express delivery is faster and prioritized.
Do I need a subscription to use your delivery services? No subscription is required — you can use our services as needed.
Can I change my delivery options if I'm not satisfied? Yes, you can modify your delivery preferences before the shipment is finalized.
Is my information and delivery history kept confidential with Express? Yes, we protect your data and delivery history with strict confidentiality and security measures.
